About Us

At Milky Way Tea House, we are passionate about crafting the perfect cup of milk tea that takes you on a journey through the cosmos of flavors. Established in 2010 by Lady, who shared a deep love for milk tea and a dream of bringing a taste of the stars to their community.

Our journey began in a small corner shop tucked away in the bustling streets of downtown. With dedication and a commitment to quality, we experimented tirelessly with different tea blends, sourced the finest ingredients, and perfected our signature recipes.

As word spread of our out-of-this-world beverages, our humble shop quickly became a beloved destination for milk tea enthusiasts seeking an escape from the ordinary. From classic flavors like Jasmine and Earl Grey to innovative creations like Milky Classic Tea and choices from her companions, each sip is a celestial experience that transports you to far-off galaxies.

Over the years, Milky Way Tea House has grown beyond our wildest dreams. Today, we proudly serve our cosmic concoctions in multiple locations across the galaxy, but our mission remains the same: to inspire wonder and delight with every cup.

Join us on this cosmic voyage as we continue to explore new horizons, push the boundaries of flavor, and share the magic of milk tea with the universe!
